Privacy Policy for Amazon Review Guardian

Effective Date: August 6, 2025

Amazon Review Guardian does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

  • No user data is stored or transmitted to any external servers.
  • The extension operates entirely within the user's browser.
  • The extension only modifies content on Amazon product pages to display review trustworthiness badges.
  • No analytics, tracking scripts, or cookies are used.

If you have any questions, you may contact the developer via the support page on the Chrome Web Store listing.
