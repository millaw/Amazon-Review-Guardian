Amazon Review Guardian

Grades Amazon reviews (A–F) based on content and trust signals.

Grading Criteria

  • Verified purchase: +2 points
  • Review length > 300 chars: +2 points
  • Review length > 100 chars: +1 point
  • Extreme rating (1 or 5 stars): –1 point
  • Neutral rating (3 stars): +1 point
  • Negative words (scam, fake, broke...): –2 points
  • Positive words (love, perfect...): +1 point

Grade Legend

A Trustworthy B Likely good C Average D Suspicious F Untrustworthy

How This Helps

This extension helps you avoid fake or low-quality reviews by assigning trust grades to each one. No scraping. No personal data collected. Just clearer decisions.
